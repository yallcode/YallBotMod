# YallBot.

A macro recorder and bot for Geometry Dash!

## Features

- 🔴 **Record** your inputs frame-perfectly as you play
- ▶️ **Replay** your recorded run with perfect accuracy
- 💾 **Auto-save** macros to disk in `.ybot` format
- 🎮 Works in the **Pause Menu** — toggle recording/playback mid-level
- ⚙️ Settings to show/hide the UI button

## How to Use

1. Start a level
2. **Pause** the game
3. Tap **YallBot.** at the bottom of the pause menu
4. Choose to **Record** a new run, or **Playback** a saved one
5. Your macro auto-saves when you stop recording!

## Notes

- Macros are saved to your Geode save folder under `macros/`
- Playback resets automatically on death/restart
- Works for both Player 1 and Player 2 (2-player levels)
